### Writing / Blog
Go to **Content → Writing** to add new articles.

Each article has:
- **Title** — appears as the article headline
- **Slug** — the URL (auto-generated from title, you can edit it)
- **Published date** — controls sort order
- **Body** — rich text editor (bold, italic, paragraphs, links)
- **Excerpt** — the 1–2 sentence preview on the Writing index page
- **SEO title + description** — what appears in Google results

To publish: write the article, then click **Publish** in the top-right corner.

## How to edit — step by step

1. Go to [martinarink.sanity.studio](https://martinarink.sanity.studio)
2. Click the document type you want to edit in the left sidebar
3. Click the document to open it
4. Edit the field you want to change
5. Click **Publish** in the top-right corner
6. The live site updates within 60 seconds

---

## Visual editing (click-to-edit on the live site)

Your site also supports **Presentation Tool** — you can click directly on text on the live site to edit it.

1. Go to your Sanity studio
2. Click **Presentation** in the left sidebar (globe icon)
3. The live site loads inside the studio
4. Click any underlined/highlighted text to edit it inline
5. Publish as normal

## What you CANNOT edit in Sanity (needs a developer)

- Page layout and structure
- Adding new pages
- Changing fonts, colors, or spacing
- Changing the assessment questions
- Brevo/email automations
- Application form questions
- Calendly / booking settings

For any of these, contact your developer.

## If something looks wrong after you publish

1. Hard refresh your browser: `Ctrl + Shift + R` (Windows) or `Cmd + Shift + R` (Mac)
2. Wait 60 seconds and try again — Vercel's CDN takes a moment to update
3. If still broken, go back into Sanity and check the field you edited
